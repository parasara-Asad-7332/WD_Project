(1)Taj Mahel

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world. It is located in Agra, India. The Taj Mahal was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a true symbol of love and devotion.The monument is made of beautiful white marble, which shines in sunlight and moonlight. The color of the Taj Mahal changes at different times of the day, making it look very attractive. It is surrounded by green gardens and water channelsThe Taj Mahal has a large central dome and four tall minaretsInside, there are fine carvings and designs made with precious stones. The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the WorldEvery year, many tourists visit the Taj Mahal from all over the world.It shows the rich history and culture of India. The Taj Mahal makes people feel amazed and proud of India.


(2)Rajasthan

The Jaisalmer Fort, also known as the Golden Fort, is one of the most beautiful forts in India.It is located in the city of Jaisalmer, Rajasthan, in the middle of the Thar Desert. The fort is built with yellow sandstone, which shines like gold during sunset and at night.The fort is built on a high hill and has strong walls and large gates.In the evening, when lights are turned on, the fort looks very bright and magical. Below the fort, there are many small houses and narrow streets that show the traditional desert life.Inside the fort, there are temples, palaces, markets, and homes. Many people still live inside the fort, which makes it a living fortTourists from all over the world visit this place to enjoy its beauty and history.The Jaisalmer Fort shows the bravery of Rajput kings and the rich culture of RajasthanIt is a symbol of heritage, pride, and beauty of India.


(3)Red Fort

The Lal Qila, also known as the Red Fort, is a famous historical monument located in Delhi, India. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in the year 1648. The fort is made of strong red sandstone, which gives it its name. In old times, the Red Fort was the main residence of Mughal emperors. It has high walls, large gates, and beautiful palaces. Important halls inside the fort are Diwan-e-Aam and Diwan-e-Khas, where the emperor met common people and special guests. The fort shows the fine art and architecture of the Mughal period with gardens, halls, and royal buildings. The Red Fort is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Every year on 15 August, the Prime Minister of India hoists the national flag here. The Red Fort is a symbol of India freedom, history, and pride.


(4)Golden Temple

The Suvarna Mandir, also known as the Golden Temple, is the most sacred religious place of Sikhs. It is located in Amritsar, Punjab. The temple was built by the fifth Sikh Guru, Guru Arjan Dev Ji, and later decorated with gold by Maharaja Ranjit Singh. The Golden Temple is built in the middle of a holy water tank called the Amrit Sarovar, which means Pool of Nectar. The shining golden dome and white marble structure make the temple very beautiful. The temple has four doors, showing the message of equality and openness for people of all religions.


(5)Charminar

The Charminar, also known as the Four Minarets, is a famous historical monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana. It was built in the year 1591 by the ruler Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah. The Charminar was built to celebrate the end of a deadly plague and also to mark the founding of the city of Hyderabad. The monument is made of granite and limestone and has a beautiful Islamic architectural style. It has four tall minarets, one on each corner, which gives the monument its name. Each minaret has a spiral staircase inside. The Charminar stands in the heart of the city and is surrounded by busy markets and old streets, including the famous Laad Bazaar, known for bangles and traditional items. At the top of the monument, there is a small mosque which is still used for prayers. The Charminar is a symbol of Hyderabad history, culture, and pride and attracts many tourists from all over India and the world.


(6)Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity is the tallest statue in the world and is located in Kevadia, Gujarat, India. It was built in honor of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, who is known as the Iron Man of India. The statue was inaugurated in 2018 and has a height of 182 meters, which represents the number of seats in the Gujarat Legislative Assembly. The statue stands near the Narmada River, close to the Sardar Sarovar Dam. It is made of bronze cladding over a steel and concrete structure.
