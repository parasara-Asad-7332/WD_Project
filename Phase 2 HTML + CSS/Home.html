Project Overview

India is a large and beautiful country in South Asia. It is known for its rich history, culture, and traditions. India has many languages, religions, and festivals. People of different cultures live together peacefully. The capital of India is New Delhi. India is famous for the Taj Mahal, which is a symbol of love. The national flag has three colors: saffron, white, and green. India has mountains, rivers, deserts, and beaches. The Ganga is a holy river. Indian food is very tasty and spicy. India is also known for yoga and Ayurveda.
