Tourist places


India Gate

  1. Located in New Delhi
  2. War memorial monument
  3. Designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens
  4. Built in 1921 and completed in 1931
  5. Height is 42 meters
  6. Built to honor 70,000 Indian soldiers
  7. Amar Jawan Jyoti flame is below the gate
  8. Famous tourist attraction in India

Taj Mahel

  1. Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh
  2. Famous historical monument of India
  3. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan
  4. Built in memory of Mumtaz Mahal
  5. Construction started in 1632
  6. Made of white marble
  7. UNESCO World Heritage Site
  8. One of the Seven Wonders of the World

Lal Qila (Red Fort)

  1. Located in Delhi
  2. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan
  3. Construction completed in 1648
  4. Made of red sandstone
  5. UNESCO World Heritage Site
  6. Prime Minister hoists flag on Independence Day
  7. Important historical monument of India
  8. Popular tourist attraction



Statue of Unity

  1. Located in Gujarat
  2. Statue of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
  3. World's tallest statue
  4. Height is 182 meters
  5. Inaugurated in 2018
  6. Built near Narmada River
  7. Major tourist attraction
  8. Symbol of national unity
